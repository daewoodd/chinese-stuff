1. Jīn tiān nǐ xiǎng zuò shén me? 

1 Reply: Jīn tiān wǒ xiǎng kàn shū.

2. Yī gè bēi zi duō shǎo qián? (How much does one cup cost?) 

2 Reply: Yī gè bēi zi wǔ shí kuài qián.

3. Nǐ men xué xiào yǒu duō shǎo lǎo shī? (How many teachers does your (plural) school have?) 

3 Reply: Wǒ men xué xiào yǒu sì shí gè lǎo shi.

4. Nǐ de péng you xiǎng chī shén me? (What would your friend like to eat?) 

4 Reply: Wǒ de péng you xiǎng chī mǐ fàn. (My friend would like to eat rice (meal).)

5. Jīn tiān jǐ hào?

5 Reply: Jīn tiān shì èr líng èr sì nián qī yuè bā rì.
